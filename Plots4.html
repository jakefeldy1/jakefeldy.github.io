City Lattitude vs. Wind Speed

This page shows the City Lattitude vs. Wind Speed plot

As cities start to get further away from the equator, there are various cities that increase in wind speed. However, typically wind speeds are not very high, with only one city having a wind speed that was higher than 35 mph. The majority of cities had a wind speed that fell between 0 and 10 mph.
