City Lattitude vs. Cloudiness

This page shows the City Lattitude vs. Cloudiness plot

There is no correlation between cloudiness and distance to or from the equator. There are multiple cities close to the equator with a low percentage of cloudiness and there are also multiple cities close to the equator with a high percentage of cloudiness. There are also cities that are far from the equator that have a low percentage of cloudiness and other groups of cities that are far from the equator but have a high percentage of cloudiness.
