City Lattitude vs. Max Temperature

This page shows the City Lattitude vs. Max Temperature plot

This graph shows that the closer to the equator that one is, the hotter the temperature is going to be. There are a few outliers on both the postive and negative lattitude side, however, the general case is that the higher temperatures occur when the city is closer to the equator.
